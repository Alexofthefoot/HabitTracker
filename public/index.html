Current Projects

Here are a few of the projects I'm currently working on:

Project 1: Tarot

A tool to generate and track tarot card readings over time, with insights on trends and reoccuring themes.personalized experience for self-reflection and growth. It serves as both a record-keeping system and a tool for deeper engagement with tarot practices.

Project 2: Online Wardrobe

A tool to monitor one's wardrobe. What gets worn and what doesn't, and how much each article costs per wear over time. Also will act as a centralized place to recordany special care and washing instructions.

Project 3: Online Bullet Journal Companion

Taking the place of a physical bullet journal, this will assist in the tracking and development of various daily and weekly habits.

Past Projects
